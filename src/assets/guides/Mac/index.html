Details

  • If the MacBook is on, press and hold the power button until it turns off.
  • Press and hold the command and R keys while pressing the power button to turn on the MacBook.
  • Keep holding the command and R keys down until the Apple logo appears.
  • When macOS Utilities appears, Select the WiFi symbol along the top menu bar and click on the store WiFi.
  • Enter the store WiFi password and click Join.
  • Select Disk Utility from the macOS Utilities and click Continue.
  • Select the partition on the Internal hard disk and click Erase.
  • Enter the name "Macintosh HD" and click Erase.
  • When the erase is finished, click Done.
  • Click the red X to exit the Disk Utility.
  • Select Reinstall macOS and click Continue.
  • Click Continue and Continue again.
  • Click Agree and Agree again.
  • Select Macintosh HD and click Install.
  • When the Welcome screen appears, press and hold the power button until prompted to shut down.
  • Click Shut Down.
