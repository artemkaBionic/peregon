Model: DELL Inspiron 23 5348

Details

  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F2 key until the system setup appears.
  • With a mouse click Load Defaults or with a keyboard press TAB twice to highlight Load Defaults and press ENTER.
  • Press ENTER to select "OK" or "Yes" to any confirmation prompts that appear.
  • With a mouse click Advanced Boot Options or with a keyboard press the DOWN ARROW three times to highlight Advanced Boot Options.
  • If there is a check mark in the box next to Enable Legacy Option ROMs then with a mouse uncheck Enable Legacy Option ROMs or with a keyboard press TAB once and press ENTER to uncheck Enable Legacy Option ROMs.
  • With a mouse click the plus (+) next to System Configuration and then click Integrated NIC or with a keyboard press TAB until the focus is in the navigation pane (the highlight will turn blue), press the DOWN ARROW until System Configuration is highlighted, press the RIGHT ARROW to expand the category, and then press the DOWN ARROW to highlight Integrated NIC.
  • If there is NOT a check mark in the box next to Enable UEFI Network Stack then with a mouse check Enable UEFI Network Stack or with a keyboard press TAB once and press ENTER to check Enable UEFI Network Stack.
  • With a mouse click the plus (+) next to Secure Boot and then click Secure Boot Enable or with a keyboard press TAB until the focus is in the navigation pane (the highlight will turn blue), press the DOWN ARROW until Secure Boot is highlighted, press the RIGHT ARROW to expand the category, and then press the DOWN ARROW to highlight Secure Boot Enable.
  • If Disabled is selected then with a mouse click Enabled or with a keyboard press TAB once, press the DOWN ARROW, and then press ENTER to select Enabled.
  • With a mouse click Apply or with a keyboard press TAB until Apply is highlighted and press ENTER
  • Press the ESC key to power off the computer.
  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F12 key until the Boot Device Menu appears.
  • Select Onboard NIC(IPV4) and press ENTER.
  • When the Refresh Environment has loaded, follow the instructions to start the refresh.
  • After the refresh is finished, click the OK button to turn off the computer.
