Windows 8 and Later

Many computers designed for Windows 8 or later do not have a sticker designating the version of Windows. If the computer has a touch screen or was manufactured after 2012, then it is most likely designed for Windows 8 or greater. Choose the system Manufacturer for instructions to perform a refresh.

  • ASUS
  • Dell
  • Hewlett Packard (HP)
  • Toshiba

Windows 7 and Earlier

Many computers designed for Windows 7 or earlier have a sticker designating the version of Windows. If the computer was manufactured before 2012, then it is most likely designed for Windows 7 or lower. Choose the system Manufacturer for instructions to perform a refresh.

  • Dell
  • Hewlett Packard (HP)
  • Toshiba
