Model: DELL Inspiron 17 5767

Details

  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F2 key until the system setup appears.
  • With a mouse click Restore Settings or with a keyboard press TAB twice to highlight Restore Settings and press ENTER.
  • Press ENTER to select "OK" or "Yes" to any confirmation prompts that appear.
  • With a mouse expand System Configuration and click Integrated NIC or with a keyboard press the DOWN ARROW six times and press ENTER to highlight System Configuration, then press the DOWN ARROW once to highlight Integrated NIC.
  • With a mouse check Enable UEFI Network Stack or with a keyboard press TAB once and press ENTER to check Enable UEFI Network Stack.
  • With a mouse click Apply or with a keyboard press TAB until Apply is highlighted and press ENTER
  • Press ENTER to select "OK" or "Yes" to any confirmation prompts that appear.
  • Press the ESC key to power off the computer.
  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F12 key until the Boot Device Menu appears.
  • Select Onboard NIC(IPV4) and press ENTER.
  • When the Refresh Environment has loaded, follow the instructions to start the refresh.
  • After the refresh is finished, click the OK button to turn off the computer.
