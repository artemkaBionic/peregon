Disable Screen Lock

  1. Press and hold the Volume Down and Power buttons until the device resets.
  2. Press and hold the Volume Up, Power, and Home buttons until the "Android system recovery" appears.
  3. Use the Volume Down button to highlight wipe data/factory reset and press the Power button.
  4. Use the Volume Down button to highlight Yes -- delete all user data and press the Power button.
  5. When the device says "Data wipe complete.", highlight reboot system now and press the Power button.
  6. Follow the instructions for first time setup.

Remove Google Account

  1. Press and hold the Volume Down and Power buttons until the device resets.
  2. Press and hold the Volume Up, Power, and Home buttons until the "Android system recovery" appears.
  3. Use the Volume Down button to highlight wipe data/factory reset and press the Power button.
  4. Use the Volume Down button to highlight Yes -- delete all user data and press the Power button.
  5. When the device says "Data wipe complete.", highlight reboot system now and press the Power button.
  6. Follow the instructions for first time setup.

Connect Wi-Fi

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Tap Wi-Fi
  3. Make sure the On/Off switch is in the On position.
  4. Tap the network name and enter the password.

Connect Wi-Fi to unlock siperLock

  1. If the device is locked, press and hold the Volume Down and Power buttons until the device resets.
  2. Open the Settings app.
  3. Tap Wi-Fi
  4. Make sure the On/Off switch is in the On position.
  5. Tap the network name and enter the password.

  1. If the device is locked, use the Wi-Fi option to connect to the network.

Enable USB Debugging on the Android Device

  1. Open Settings > About on your Android device. Depending on the Android version it could be:
    • Settings > More > About or
    • Settings > General > About
  2. Scroll to Build number and tap it 7 times to enable developer mode.
  3. Press the Back button to return to the Settings menu and select Developer Options.
  4. Enable USB Debugging
