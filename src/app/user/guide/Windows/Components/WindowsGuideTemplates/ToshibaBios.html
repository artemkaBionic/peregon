Follow these steps to refresh DELL (Windows 7 and earlier):

1. Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F2 key until "Press <F2> to enter SETUP" appears.

2. Press the F9 key to load setup defaults, then select YES and press ENTER to confirm.

3. Press the F10 key to save changes and exit, then select Yes and press ENTER to confirm.

4. While rebooting, repeatedly press the F12 key until the Boot Menu appears.

5. Select LAN and press ENTER.

6. When the Refresh Environment has loaded, follow the instructions to start the refresh.

7. After the refresh is finished, click the OK button to turn off the computer.
