Follow this steps to refresh laptop:

  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F9 key.
  • When the boot menu appears, select "USB Hard Drive (UEFI)" and press Enter. When the Refresh Environment has loaded, follow the instructions to start the refresh.
  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F12 key.
  • When the boot menu appears, select "USB1 - UEFI OS" and press Enter. When the Refresh Environment has loaded, follow the instructions to start the refresh.
