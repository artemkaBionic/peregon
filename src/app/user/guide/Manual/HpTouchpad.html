Follow this steps to refresh HP Touchpad:

1. Press the Center Button to bring up the Launcher

2. Tap on the Settings tab of the Launcher

3. Tap Device Info and then select Reset Options

4. Tap Secure Full Erase and confirm by tapping Secure Full Erase again
