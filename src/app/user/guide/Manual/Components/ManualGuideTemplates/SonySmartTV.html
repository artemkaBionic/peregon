Follow this steps to refresh Sony Smart TV:

1. Plug out TV power cord from electrical outlet for a minute;

2. Plug in TV power cord to electrical outlet. TV will automatically turn on;

3. Press and hold button on remote control;

4. While holding button on remote, tab the Power button on side of the TV;

5. Wait until 'Reset' message is displayed on the bottom left side of the screen;

6. Release button on remote control and wait for TV to complete the reset;


7. If after going through steps device rebooted with factory settings then refresh was successful.
