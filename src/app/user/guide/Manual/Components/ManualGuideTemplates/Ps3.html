Is refresh successful?

Follow these steps to refresh PlayStation 3:

1. Turn on PlayStation 3.

2. Go to Settings.

3. Drop down to System Settings.

4. Select Restore PS3 Settings.

5. Continue to follow the screen instructions.

6. When the restore process has started do not turn off or interrupt the process.

7. If the PlayStation 3 reboots with factory settings, then refresh was successful.
