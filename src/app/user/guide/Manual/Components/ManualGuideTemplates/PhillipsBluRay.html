Follow these steps to refresh Phillips Blu-Ray disc player:

1. Turn on Phillips Blu-Ray disc player.

2. Connect the Phillips Blu-Ray disc player to a TV so you can see the Blu-Ray disc player menu.

3. On the Home Screen of the Blu-Ray disc player, go to Setup -> Advanced ->Restore Default Settings.

4. If the blu-ray player reboots with factory settings, then refresh was successful.
