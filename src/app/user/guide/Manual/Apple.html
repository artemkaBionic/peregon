Is refresh successful?

Follow this steps to refresh Apple device:

1. Open iTunes on your Mac or PC;

2. Connect your iPhone, iPad, or iPod to your computer with the cable that came with your device;

3. In the Summary panel, click Restore Apple device;

4. Click Restore again to confirm;

5. After a restore, the iOS device restarts. You should then see Slide to set up. Follow the steps in the iOS Setup Assistant.

Is refresh successful?


Inspect the device and make sure that:

  • Device turns on;
  • Screen and casing are in good condition;
  • Touchscreen and buttons are working;
