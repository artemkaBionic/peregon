Follow this steps to refresh Samsung Blu-Ray player:

1. Turn on Samsung Blu-Ray player;

2. Press the STOP button for more than five seconds with no disc in the drive. The initialization message appears on the screen, and the player turns off automatically;

3. When you turn your product on again, all the settings will be reset and all user information will be erased.

Is refresh successful?


Inspect the device and make sure that:

  • Device turns on;
  • Screen and casing are in good condition;
  • Buttons are working;
