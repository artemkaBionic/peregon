7360D04

Model: DELL

Details

  • Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F2 key until "F2 Setup" appears.
  • Click on the button LOAD DEFAULTS
  • Select/Click YES to confirm selection.
  • Navigate to GENERAL/BOOT SEQUENCE.
  • Check ONBOARD NIC and click APPLY
  • Navigate to SYSTEM CONFIGURATION/INTEGRATED NIC
  • Check ENABLE w/PXE and click APPLY
  • Click EXIT to close the BIOS configuration.
  • While rebooting, repeatedly press the F12 key until the boot selection screen appears
  • Select ONBOARD NIC and press ENTER
  • When the Refresh Environment has loaded, follow the instructions to start the refresh.
  • After the refresh is finished, click the OK button to turn off the computer.
