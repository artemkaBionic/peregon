Follow these steps to refresh Sharp TV:

1. Turn on Sharp TV.

2. Unplug the AC cord of the TV.

3. Press and hold the [INPUT] and [VOL-] buttons on the TV itself.

4. While continuing to hold those buttons, plug the AC cord in.

5. Wait until the Sharp logo appears, then let go of the buttons.

6. After the TV completely boots up, a 'K' should appear in the upper left corner of the screen.

7. Once you see the 'K', press the buttons [VOL-] and [CH-] together one time (press them both together and let go).

8. This should open the Service Menu of the TV.

9. Using the remote control, press the Down arrow one time (to move to the 2nd page of the service menu).

10. With the Industry INIT row highlighted, press Enter on the remote. This will start the resetting process.

11. When complete, the screen should turn green, indicating it was successful.
